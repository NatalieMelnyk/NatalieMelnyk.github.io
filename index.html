Features

Access your Tasks anywhere you go with our web task manager! Create your own to-do lists with the power of our intelligent reminders and seamless organization.
Daily To-do

Get an overview of your day, everyday, with the Daily to-do list. Designed to strip away the noise and allow you to focus on what matters for the day. Each morning, the list is populated with your scheduled deadlines and overdue items. Allowing you to transform that intimidating backlog  into a an actionable checklist.

Set Reminders

Your own digital safety net, ensuring high-priority tasks never slip through the cracks. Beyond simple time-based alerts, users can configure multi-channel notifications—including browser push alerts, desktop pings, or even mobile nudges—delivered exactly when and where they're needed most. This keeps you in your flow state until the moment you need to spring into action.

Easy Organization

Color code and create custom categories for your tasks. You can slice through your workload using any criteria you choose, such as "Work," "Personal," or "High Energy." By minimizing the friction of sorting, the system ensures that organizing your life feels less like a chore and more like a strategy session.
